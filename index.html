CDF Labs provides University of Toronto students find which CDF computer labs have the most computers available! The app was inspired by Eugene Cheung's Android version.

The app is open source, please feel free to fork, improve and send a pull request or open any issues. You can reach out to me on twitter if you have any feedback.
